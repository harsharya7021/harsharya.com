Kochi.

A few rolls shot in Fort Kochi over a long weekend — heritage doorways, fishing boats hauled up at dusk, the green of a window framed by tamarind, a Kathakali performer holding a single beat under a stage light. Sequenced the way I remember the trip, not the way it happened.
